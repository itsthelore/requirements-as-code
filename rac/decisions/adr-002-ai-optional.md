# ADR-002 AI Optional

## Status

Accepted

## Context

RAC should be useful on its own, with AI as an enhancement rather than a dependency.

## Decision

RAC must provide value without AI.

## Consequences

### Positive

- Lower complexity
- Easier testing
- Works offline
