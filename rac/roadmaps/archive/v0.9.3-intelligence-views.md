# RAC v0.9.3 — Intelligence Views

## Problem

RAC understands repository knowledge through:

- Artifacts
- Relationships
- Validation
- History

Explorer currently exposes individual views.

Users need higher-level understanding of how knowledge evolves.

---

## Outcomes

Introduce advanced repository intelligence experiences.

Help users answer:

```text
Why does this exist?

What depends on this?

How did we get here?
```

---

## Initiatives

## Initiative 1 — Impact View

Show downstream dependencies.

Example:

```text
Impact of ADR-004

Affected:

REQ-001
REQ-007
PROMPT-003
```

---

## Initiative 2 — Knowledge Graph View

Visualize artifact relationships.

Example:

```text
ROADMAP-Q3

    ↓

REQ-001

    ↓

ADR-004
```

---

## Initiative 3 — Artifact Lineage

Show lifecycle.

Example:

```text
REQ-001

Created

Updated

Superseded By
```

---

## Initiative 4 — Repository Timeline

Show knowledge evolution.

Example:

```text
March

REQ-001 created

April

ADR-004 accepted
```

---

## Initiative 5 — Domain Views

Navigate by product area.

Example:

```text
Payments

Requirements 12
Decisions     4
Roadmaps      1
```

---

## Requirements

## REQ-001 — Intelligence From Core

Explorer shall visualize intelligence.

It shall not create intelligence.

---

## REQ-002 — Relationship Driven

Views shall use RAC relationship models.

---

## REQ-003 — Repository Scale

Views shall support large repositories.

---

## Non-Goals

- AI reasoning
- Prediction
- Automatic recommendations

---

## Success Measures

Explorer helps users understand:

```text
Repository structure

Knowledge evolution

Change impact
```

rather than only individual artifacts.
